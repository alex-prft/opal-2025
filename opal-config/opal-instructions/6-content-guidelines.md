## Updated Content Guidelines for FreshProduce.com – Opal AI Optimization

### Content Strategy Framework

#### Mission
Deliver authoritative, actionable content that positions FreshProduce.com as the premier digital resource for fresh produce professionals, driving IFPA member engagement through AI-powered personalization.

#### Objectives
1. Establish industry authority
2. Drive member engagement
3. Optimize content consumption
4. Enhance personalization effectiveness

### Content Quality Standards

#### Editorial Guidelines
- **Accuracy**: Fact-checked, expert-reviewed, source-attributed
- **Tone**: Professional, helpful, consistent with IFPA brand
- **Accessibility**: Clear language, inclusive terminology, WCAG compliance
- **Structure**: Logical flow, scannable formatting, proper heading hierarchy

#### Freshness Scoring
- Content older than 6 months flagged for review
- Engagement decay triggers update recommendations
- Freshness index calculated based on last update and interaction trends

#### Semantic Alignment
- Content scored for relevance to IFPA personas:
  - Strategic Buyer Sarah
  - Innovation-Focused Frank
  - Compliance-Conscious Carol
  - Executive Leader Linda
- Semantic topic analysis ensures alignment with member needs

### Personalization Strategy

#### Dynamic Content Blocks
- Homepage and article personalization based on persona behavior
- CTA optimization per segment

#### Content Recommendations
- AI-driven suggestions based on:
  - Topic gaps
  - Underutilized high-engagement themes
  - Seasonal relevance

### Content Types & Guidelines

#### Blog Articles
- Headline: 50–60 characters, primary keyword
- Meta: 150–160 characters, CTA included
- Body: 800–2000 words, subheadings, visuals, internal/external links
- Author Bio: 50 words, credibility signal

#### Resource Content
- Executive summary, key findings, detailed analysis, recommendations
- Gated access for member-exclusive content

#### Event Content
- Pre-event: Speaker spotlights, session previews
- Post-event: Summaries, interviews, trend analysis

### Performance Metrics
- Time on page: Target 3+ minutes
- Bounce rate: <40%
- Social shares and comment engagement
- Personalization effectiveness by segment

### Implementation Roadmap
- Phase 1: Core SEO, AEO, personalization blocks
- Phase 2: AI recommendations, advanced personalization
- Phase 3: Predictive modeling, automated workflows

These guidelines support the Content Review Agent’s enhanced capabilities and ensure alignment with IFPA’s strategic goals and Opal AI personalization workflows.
