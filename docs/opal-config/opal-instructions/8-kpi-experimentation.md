# FreshProduce.com - KPI & Experimentation Definitions for Opal AI

## Key Performance Indicator Framework

### Business Objective Alignment

#### Primary Business Goals
1. **Member Growth**: Increase IFPA membership acquisition and retention
2. **Engagement Enhancement**: Maximize user engagement and content consumption
3. **Revenue Optimization**: Improve conversion rates and customer lifetime value
4. **Market Leadership**: Establish digital authority in fresh produce industry
5. **Operational Efficiency**: Optimize marketing spend and resource allocation

### KPI Hierarchy Structure

```
Strategic KPIs (Board/Executive Level)
├── Tactical KPIs (Marketing/Operations Level)
│   ├── Operational KPIs (Team/Campaign Level)
│   │   └── Leading Indicators (Daily/Weekly Metrics)
```

## Strategic KPIs (Monthly/Quarterly Reporting)

### Member Growth Metrics

#### Member Acquisition Rate (MAR)
- **Definition**: Net new members acquired per month
- **Calculation**: (New Members - Cancelled Members) / Month
- **Target**: 5% month-over-month growth
- **Opal Integration**: AI prediction of acquisition potential by traffic source
- **Reporting**: Executive dashboard, monthly board reports

#### Member Conversion Rate (MCR)
- **Definition**: Percentage of website visitors who become members
- **Calculation**: (New Members / Total Unique Visitors) × 100
- **Target**: 2.5% overall, 8% for qualified leads
- **Opal Integration**: AI optimization of conversion funnel and touchpoints
- **Segmentation**: By traffic source, persona, campaign

#### Member Lifetime Value (MLV)
- **Definition**: Total revenue generated by a member over their lifetime
- **Calculation**: (Average Annual Fee × Average Retention Years) + Additional Revenue
- **Target**: $2,500 baseline, 15% annual improvement
- **Opal Integration**: AI prediction of member value and retention probability
- **Cohort Analysis**: By acquisition channel, member type, engagement level

#### Member Retention Rate (MRR)
- **Definition**: Percentage of members who renew their membership
- **Calculation**: (Renewed Members / Eligible for Renewal) × 100
- **Target**: 85% annual retention rate
- **Opal Integration**: AI early warning system for churn risk
- **Segmentation**: By member tenure, engagement level, member type

### Revenue Performance Metrics

#### Revenue Growth Rate (RGR)
- **Definition**: Year-over-year revenue growth from all sources
- **Calculation**: ((Current Year Revenue - Previous Year Revenue) / Previous Year Revenue) × 100
- **Target**: 12% annual growth
- **Components**: Membership fees, event revenue, sponsorship, services
- **Opal Integration**: AI revenue forecasting and opportunity identification

#### Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)
- **Definition**: Total marketing cost to acquire one new member
- **Calculation**: Total Marketing Spend / New Members Acquired
- **Target**: <$200 per member (blended), <$150 (organic/referral)
- **Opal Integration**: AI optimization of marketing spend allocation
- **Channel Breakdown**: Paid advertising, content marketing, events, partnerships

#### Return on Marketing Investment (ROMI)
- **Definition**: Revenue generated per dollar of marketing spend
- **Calculation**: (Revenue from Marketing - Marketing Spend) / Marketing Spend
- **Target**: 4:1 return ratio (400% ROI)
- **Opal Integration**: AI attribution modeling and budget optimization
- **Attribution Window**: 90-day attribution for complex B2B sales cycles

## Tactical KPIs (Weekly/Monthly Management)

### Website Performance Metrics

#### Organic Traffic Growth (OTG)
- **Definition**: Month-over-month growth in organic search traffic
- **Calculation**: ((Current Month Organic Traffic - Previous Month) / Previous Month) × 100
- **Target**: 8% monthly growth
- **Opal Integration**: AI content optimization and SEO strategy recommendations
- **Keyword Tracking**: Position improvements for target keywords

#### Content Engagement Score (CES)
- **Definition**: Composite score of content performance across multiple metrics
- **Calculation**: Weighted average of time on page, bounce rate, social shares, conversions
- **Formula**: (Time on Page × 0.3) + (Bounce Rate × 0.2) + (Social Shares × 0.2) + (Conversions × 0.3)
- **Target**: Score >75 (scale 0-100)
- **Opal Integration**: AI content performance prediction and optimization suggestions

#### Lead Generation Rate (LGR)
- **Definition**: Number of qualified leads generated per month
- **Calculation**: Total Qualified Leads / Month
- **Target**: 500 qualified leads per month
- **Lead Qualification**: Email signup + professional information + engagement threshold
- **Opal Integration**: AI lead scoring and qualification automation

#### Email Performance Metrics
```
Open Rate (OR):
- Definition: Percentage of emails opened
- Calculation: (Emails Opened / Emails Delivered) × 100
- Target: >25% (industry average: 21.33%)
- Opal Integration: AI subject line optimization

Click-Through Rate (CTR):
- Definition: Percentage of email recipients who click links
- Calculation: (Total Clicks / Emails Delivered) × 100
- Target: >3.5% (industry average: 2.62%)
- Opal Integration: AI content personalization

Conversion Rate (CR):
- Definition: Percentage of email clicks resulting in desired action
- Calculation: (Conversions / Total Clicks) × 100
- Target: >12%
- Opal Integration: AI landing page optimization
```

### Personalization Performance Metrics

#### Personalization Uplift (PU)
- **Definition**: Performance improvement from personalized vs. generic experiences
- **Calculation**: ((Personalized Performance - Generic Performance) / Generic Performance) × 100
- **Target**: >25% uplift across key metrics
- **Measurement Areas**: CTR, conversion rate, engagement time, retention
- **Opal Integration**: AI-driven personalization strategy optimization

#### Segment Performance Index (SPI)
- **Definition**: Relative performance of different audience segments
- **Calculation**: Segment Performance / Average Performance × 100
- **Target**: All primary segments >90 index score
- **Key Segments**: Strategic buyers, producers, compliance professionals, executives
- **Opal Integration**: AI segment optimization and strategy refinement

#### AI Recommendation Accuracy (ARA)
- **Definition**: Percentage of AI content recommendations that result in engagement
- **Calculation**: (Recommended Content Engagements / Total Recommendations) × 100
- **Target**: >40% engagement rate on recommendations
- **Engagement Definition**: >30 seconds time on page or specific action taken
- **Opal Integration**: Continuous learning and recommendation algorithm improvement

## Operational KPIs (Daily/Weekly Team Management)

### Content Performance Metrics

#### Content Consumption Rate (CCR)
- **Definition**: Average number of content pieces consumed per user session
- **Calculation**: Total Content Views / Total Sessions
- **Target**: >2.5 pieces per session
- **Content Types**: Articles, resources, videos, infographics
- **Opal Integration**: AI content sequencing and user journey optimization

#### Search Engine Results Position (SERP)
- **Definition**: Average ranking position for target keywords
- **Calculation**: Sum of keyword positions / Number of tracked keywords
- **Target**: Average position <10 (first page)
- **Keyword Categories**: Brand terms, product terms, industry terms
- **Opal Integration**: AI SEO optimization and content gap identification

#### Social Media Engagement Rate (SMER)
- **Definition**: Engagement interactions relative to follower count
- **Calculation**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers × 100
- **Target**: >2% engagement rate across platforms
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn (primary), Twitter, YouTube
- **Opal Integration**: AI content timing and format optimization

### User Experience Metrics

#### Page Load Speed (PLS)
- **Definition**: Average time for pages to fully load
- **Measurement**: Core Web Vitals, Google PageSpeed Insights
- **Target**: <2 seconds average load time
- **Critical Pages**: Homepage, member portal, registration forms
- **Opal Integration**: Performance impact monitoring of personalization features

#### Mobile Optimization Score (MOS)
- **Definition**: Mobile user experience performance rating
- **Measurement**: Google Mobile-Friendly Test, user behavior analytics
- **Target**: >95% mobile compatibility score
- **Key Metrics**: Mobile conversion rate, bounce rate, session duration
- **Opal Integration**: Mobile-specific personalization optimization

#### Form Completion Rate (FCR)
- **Definition**: Percentage of users who complete forms after starting
- **Calculation**: (Form Completions / Form Starts) × 100
- **Target**: >60% for membership forms, >80% for newsletter signup
- **Form Types**: Membership registration, content downloads, event registration
- **Opal Integration**: AI form optimization and progressive profiling

## Leading Indicators (Real-Time Monitoring)

### Traffic Quality Indicators

#### Session Quality Score (SQS)
- **Definition**: Composite score indicating visitor engagement quality
- **Components**: Session duration, pages per session, bounce rate, conversion actions
- **Calculation**: Weighted algorithm based on engagement behaviors
- **Target**: Continuous improvement, benchmark against industry standards
- **Opal Integration**: Real-time personalization adjustments based on session quality

#### Intent Signal Strength (ISS)
- **Definition**: Likelihood that a visitor will take a desired action
- **Measurement**: Behavioral scoring algorithm incorporating multiple signals
- **Signals**: Page views, time on site, content downloads, form interactions
- **Target**: Identify high-intent visitors for personalized experiences
- **Opal Integration**: AI intent prediction and dynamic content delivery

#### Conversion Funnel Velocity (CFV)
- **Definition**: Speed at which leads progress through the conversion funnel
- **Measurement**: Average time between funnel stages
- **Stages**: Awareness → Interest → Consideration → Decision → Action
- **Target**: Reduce average funnel completion time by 20%
- **Opal Integration**: AI funnel optimization and acceleration tactics

## Experimentation Framework

### A/B Testing Standards

#### Test Design Requirements
1. **Hypothesis Formation**: Clear, measurable prediction of expected outcomes
2. **Statistical Power**: Minimum 80% power to detect meaningful differences
3. **Sample Size**: Calculated based on baseline conversion rate and expected lift
4. **Test Duration**: Minimum 2 weeks, maximum 8 weeks for statistical validity
5. **Success Metrics**: Primary and secondary KPIs defined before test launch

#### Test Categories and Examples

#### Website Optimization Tests
```
Homepage Personalization:
- Variable: Hero message and content blocks by persona
- Hypothesis: Persona-specific content increases conversion by 25%
- Primary Metric: Member signup rate
- Secondary Metrics: Engagement time, bounce rate, content consumption

Call-to-Action Optimization:
- Variable: CTA text, color, placement, urgency language
- Hypothesis: Personalized CTAs increase click-through by 15%
- Primary Metric: Click-through rate
- Secondary Metrics: Conversion rate, form completion rate

Content Recommendation Engine:
- Variable: AI vs. manual content recommendations
- Hypothesis: AI recommendations increase engagement by 30%
- Primary Metric: Content engagement rate
- Secondary Metrics: Session duration, return visit rate
```

#### Email Marketing Tests
```
Subject Line Optimization:
- Variable: Personalization, urgency, length, emoji usage
- Hypothesis: Personalized subject lines increase opens by 20%
- Primary Metric: Email open rate
- Secondary Metrics: Click-through rate, unsubscribe rate

Send Time Optimization:
- Variable: Day of week, time of day by recipient time zone
- Hypothesis: AI-optimized send times increase engagement by 15%
- Primary Metric: Email engagement score (composite)
- Secondary Metrics: Open rate, click rate, conversion rate

Content Personalization:
- Variable: Industry-specific vs. role-specific content
- Hypothesis: Role-specific content increases conversion by 25%
- Primary Metric: Email conversion rate
- Secondary Metrics: Click-through rate, forward rate
```

### Multivariate Testing (MVT)

#### Advanced Testing Scenarios
1. **Full Page Optimization**: Test multiple page elements simultaneously
2. **Personalization Strategy**: Test different personalization approaches
3. **Content Strategy**: Test content types, formats, and presentation styles
4. **User Journey**: Test different paths through the conversion funnel

#### MVT Implementation Framework
```typescript
interface MultivariateTest {
  test_id: string;
  test_name: string;
  variables: TestVariable[];
  traffic_allocation: number; // Percentage of traffic included
  success_metrics: SuccessMetric[];
  duration: {
    start_date: string;
    end_date: string;
    minimum_duration: number; // Days
  };
  statistical_requirements: {
    confidence_level: number; // 95%
    statistical_power: number; // 80%
    minimum_detectable_effect: number; // 10%
  };
}

interface TestVariable {
  variable_name: string;
  variations: string[];
  default_variation: string;
  personalization_rules?: PersonalizationRule[];
}
```

### Opal AI Integration Testing

#### AI-Powered Experimentation
1. **Automated Hypothesis Generation**: AI suggests test ideas based on data patterns
2. **Dynamic Traffic Allocation**: AI adjusts traffic distribution based on early results
3. **Multivariate Optimization**: AI manages complex multivariate tests
4. **Personalized Testing**: AI creates different test experiences for different segments
5. **Predictive Analysis**: AI predicts test outcomes and recommends early decisions

#### AI Testing Scenarios
```
Predictive Personalization Test:
- Hypothesis: AI-predicted preferences outperform declared preferences
- Method: Compare AI recommendations vs. user-selected preferences
- Metrics: Engagement rate, conversion rate, satisfaction score

Dynamic Content Optimization:
- Hypothesis: Real-time content optimization improves performance
- Method: Static vs. dynamic content delivery based on session behavior
- Metrics: Session quality score, conversion rate, return visit rate

Automated Segment Discovery:
- Hypothesis: AI-discovered segments perform better than manual segments
- Method: AI clustering vs. traditional demographic/firmographic segmentation
- Metrics: Segment performance index, personalization uplift, engagement quality
```

- **Cross-Channel Optimization**: 20% improvement in customer journey metrics
- **Automation Efficiency**: 50% reduction in manual optimization tasks

#### Fly Phase Success (Months 18+)
- **Transformative Results**: Market-leading conversion and engagement rates

- **Technology Costs**: Platform licensing, integration, maintenance
- **Personnel Costs**: Team time, training, external consulting
- **Content Costs**: Creation, optimization, personalization development
- **Testing Costs**: Experimentation platform, statistical analysis tools
