## Primary Personas

### Persona 1: "Strategic Buyer Sarah"
**Commercial Produce Buyer - Grocery Chain**

#### Demographics
- **Age**: 35-45
- **Role**: Senior Produce Buyer / Category Manager
- **Company**: Regional grocery chain (50-200 stores)
- **Experience**: 8-12 years in produce industry
- **Location**: Major metropolitan areas

#### Professional Profile
- **Responsibilities**: Sourcing decisions, vendor relationships, pricing negotiations
- **Budget Authority**: $10M+ annual produce purchasing
- **Team Size**: 3-8 direct reports
- **Key Challenges**: Cost management, quality consistency, supply chain reliability
- **Success Metrics**: Margin improvement, customer satisfaction, inventory turnover

#### Behavioral Characteristics
- **Decision Style**: Data-driven, analytical, risk-aware
- **Information Needs**: Market trends, pricing forecasts, supplier performance
- **Technology Adoption**: Moderate to high, values efficiency tools
- **Communication Preference**: Email summaries, mobile-friendly content
- **Networking Style**: Professional conferences, supplier meetings

#### Content Preferences
- **Primary Interests**: Market intelligence, sourcing strategies, cost optimization
- **Content Types**: Research reports, case studies, webinar series
- **Consumption Patterns**: Morning industry news, weekly trend reports
- **Engagement Triggers**: ROI-focused content, peer success stories
- **Personalization Opportunities**: Region-specific data, category focus

#### Journey Mapping
1. **Awareness**: Industry publications, conference sessions
2. **Consideration**: IFPA resources, peer recommendations
3. **Evaluation**: Free reports, webinar attendance
4. **Decision**: Membership benefits analysis, ROI calculation
5. **Retention**: Ongoing value demonstration, exclusive insights

#### Opal Personalization Strategy
- **Homepage**: Market trend highlights, supplier spotlights
- **Content Recommendations**: Buying guides, negotiation strategies
- **Email Campaigns**: Weekly market updates, seasonal forecasts
- **Event Targeting**: Sourcing conferences, supplier showcases
