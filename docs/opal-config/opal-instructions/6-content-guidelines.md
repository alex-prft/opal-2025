## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Strategy

### Featured Snippet Optimization

#### Target Question Formats
1. **"What is [topic]?"** - Definitional content for industry terms
2. **"How to [process]?"** - Step-by-step guides for industry practices
3. **"Why [concept]?"** - Explanatory content for industry challenges
4. **"When [timing]?"** - Seasonal and timing-based guidance
5. **"Where [location]?"** - Geographic and regional information

#### Structured Answer Framework
```
Question: "What are the best practices for produce food safety?"

Answer Structure:
- Direct Answer (50-60 words)
- Key Points (bulleted list, 3-5 items)
- Supporting Details (2-3 paragraphs)
- Related Resources (internal links)
- Expert Attribution (credibility signal)
```

### Voice Search Optimization

#### Natural Language Patterns
- **Conversational Queries**: "How do I find reliable produce suppliers?"
- **Local Intent**: "Fresh produce associations near me"
- **Comparative**: "What's the difference between organic and conventional produce?"
- **Seasonal**: "When is peak season for [specific produce]?"

#### Answer Optimization
- **Concise Responses**: 29-word average for voice search results
- **Conversational Tone**: Natural language matching spoken queries
- **Local Context**: Geographic relevance for regional topics
- **Featured Snippet Format**: Structured for easy voice reading

### AI Search Engine Optimization

#### Content Signals for AI Engines
1. **Topical Authority**: Comprehensive coverage of produce industry topics
2. **Expertise Indicators**: Author credentials, industry experience, certifications
3. **Freshness Signals**: Regular content updates, current data, recent insights
4. **User Engagement**: Time on page, social shares, comment engagement
5. **Technical Excellence**: Fast loading, mobile optimization, structured data

#### Entity-Based SEO
- **Industry Entities**: IFPA, FDA, USDA, organic certification bodies
- **Product Entities**: Specific produce types, varieties, seasonal categories
- **Process Entities**: Food safety protocols, handling procedures, storage methods
- **Geographic Entities**: Growing regions, distribution centers, market locations

## Content Guidelines by Type

### Blog Articles

#### Structure Requirements
1. **Compelling Headline** (50-60 characters, includes primary keyword)
2. **Meta Description** (150-160 characters, includes CTA)
3. **Introduction** (100-150 words, includes keyword and value proposition)
4. **Main Content** (800-2000 words, scannable with subheadings)
5. **Conclusion** (50-100 words, includes CTA)
6. **Author Bio** (50 words, establishes expertise)

#### Content Elements
- **Data Points**: Include relevant statistics and research findings
- **Visual Elements**: Images, infographics, charts (optimized for web)
- **Internal Links**: 3-5 links to related content and resources
- **External Links**: 1-2 authoritative sources for credibility
- **Call-to-Action**: Clear next step (membership, resource download, event registration)

#### SEO Optimization Checklist
- [ ] Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, and conclusion
- [ ] Secondary keywords naturally integrated throughout
- [ ] Proper heading structure (H1, H2, H3) with keywords
- [ ] Alt text for all images with descriptive keywords
- [ ] Schema markup for articles and author information
- [ ] Internal linking to related content and key pages
- [ ] Social sharing optimization with Open Graph tags

### Resource Content (Guides, Whitepapers, Reports)

#### Format Standards
1. **Executive Summary** (200-300 words)
2. **Key Findings** (bulleted highlights)
3. **Detailed Analysis** (2000-5000 words)
4. **Actionable Recommendations** (specific next steps)
5. **Supporting Data** (charts, graphs, statistics)
6. **Expert Insights** (quotes, interviews, case studies)

#### Gating Strategy
- **Lead Magnets**: High-value content requiring email signup
- **Progressive Profiling**: Additional information for premium content
- **Member Exclusive**: Advanced resources for IFPA members only
- **Tiered Access**: Basic, premium, and executive-level content

### Event Content

#### Pre-Event Content
- **Speaker Spotlights**: Biographical content with keyword optimization
- **Session Previews**: Detailed descriptions with industry keywords
- **Industry Insights**: Trend analysis and market predictions
- **Networking Guides**: How to maximize event value

#### Post-Event Content
- **Session Summaries**: Key takeaways and insights
- **Expert Interviews**: Video and written content from speakers
- **Trend Analysis**: Event-based industry forecasting
- **Community Highlights**: Member success stories and networking outcomes
